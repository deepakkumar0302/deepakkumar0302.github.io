Teaching and Learning Scholarship

I am currently conducting a teaching and learning research project titled “Investigating the Retrieval Effect with and without AI Support: A Randomized Controlled Trial among College Students”, with Professor George Davis. This study evaluates how retrieval practice — with and without AI assistance — influences student learning outcomes and retention in economics courses. The project contributes to the growing evidence on effective teaching strategies in higher education.
